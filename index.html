About Us

Welcome to AstroStellar.

This is a unique project just because it's based only on celestial objects like comets. At the moment, we focus on learning with our community and our investors more about them!

Our main goals are tokenizing the comets, learning more things about it with our community, becoming an worldwide recognised token issuer, and participating in the funding and support of astronomical projects.

Furthermore, we would like to motivate and inspire our generation and the next ones to explore the space and maybe one day to finally answer the question if we are all alone?
